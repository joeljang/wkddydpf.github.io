About Me

I am a M.S. student in the Graduate school of AI at KAIST, advised by Minjoon Seo working on solving NLP problems. I obtained B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University. I also had research internships at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) Europe, Naver, and Kakao Brain working on different problems such as anomaly detection, hate speech detection, and large-scaled representation learning. Currently, I am focused on solving NLP problems through continual learning.
