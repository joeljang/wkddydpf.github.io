About Me

I am a M.S. student in the Graduate school of AI at KAIST, advised by Minjoon Seo working on solving NLP tasks. I obtained B.S. in Computer Science and Engineering at Korea University. I also have internship experience at Korea Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) Europe, NAVER, and KAKAO BRAIN working on different tasks such as anomaly detection, hate speech detection, and large-scaled representation learning. My main research interests includes solving NLP tasks with continual learning.
